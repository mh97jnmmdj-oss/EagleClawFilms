Founded in 2024, Eagle Claw Films is an independent production company based in CT, dedicated to producing bold, compelling, and visually stunning motion pictures and documentaries.

We believe that cinema is a profound catalyst for change. Our slate of projects is deeply rooted in illuminating stories of inequality, championing social justice, and connecting humanity through shared experiences. We are committed to amplifying voices that advocate for what is right, using the screen to challenge perspectives and spark meaningful conversations.

Specializing in both short-form and long-form storytelling, we constantly redefine our approach to filmmaking in the digital age. By seamlessly blending high-quality cinematic production with innovative social media strategies, we craft engaging, high-impact content that resonates with modern audiences across all platforms.
